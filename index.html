About Me

Akshat Jerath

Stoke-On-Trent, England

Hello! I’m Akshat Jerath, an enthusiastic and innovative Game/Level Designer with a passion for creating immersive and engaging gameplay experiences. I have a solid foundation in prototyping and designing, coupled with proficiency in various design tools and software.

I thrive in collaborative brainstorming and iterative design processes, bringing fresh insights and creative solutions to forward-thinking game development teams. My work is driven by a deep understanding of player motivations and preferences.

Outside the realm of game design, I have a few hobbies that keep life exciting. I'm an avid traveler, having explored several continents with my family. The thrill of discovering new cultures and landscapes fuels my love for adventure. Additionally, I have a soft spot for cars - an interest that adds a dash of excitement to my downtime.
