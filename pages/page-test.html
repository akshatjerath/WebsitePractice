Challenges faced and how I overcame them.

  • • I had a hard time getting around the AI with shooting the player at certain ranges. A:I had to fix the angle of the perception sight and change the delay timer that was there between player getting sighted and then the AI starts shooting. • Problems with the UI not showing the correct ammo count. A:It was a problem with wrong variable being connected and miscalculation of ammo count. • Player was unable to interact with the car and the exit location wasn’t getting updated. A:I researched and made some changes in the exit location world location and disable the second character to sit in the car. I scripted it to spawn besides the primary character when he exited the car. • There were minor problems in the level as there were too many meshes, so the level wasn’t optimized and was making the game lag. A:To fix this I remade the level by decreasing the asset count and to get rid of the lag.
